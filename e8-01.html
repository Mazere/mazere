#EB-01  - Gibson ES350 – 1948 –

The ES350 was the first cutaway electric guitar ever issued by Gibson just after WWII. Figured wood, early P90 pickups (that would eventually become THE classic single-coil unit for decades) seduced many Jazz guitarists such as Barney Kessel.

This fully original instrument in excellent condition was built late 48 or early 49. It is a great example of the impeccable Gibson quality construction in those times.

Logs in the background are meant to evocate the very ‘wood’ tonal and construction of this guitar.
