#F4-01 - Gretsch 6128 Duo-Jet – 1954 –

To compete with the Gibson Les Paul, Gretsch issued the single cutaway Jet series in early 1954.
This fully original example was built in early 1954 and still bears the script logo soon to be replaced with the block-letter logo as seen in the Gretsch 1955 catalog,

This is the first variant of this model with Serial  Number 11912

The old farm trailer from the 50’s on which the guitar is resting, displays cool faded colors nicely matching this great American vintage piece

Here is a fine demonstration of this guitar by Greg from Wildwood Guitars. The instrument he his using is as close as it can be from ours – the serial number is only 8 numbers apart : #11920 vs. #11912 !!
